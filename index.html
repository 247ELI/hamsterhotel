Welcome to a hamster’s paradise

Nestled in the heart of Costa California, La Petite Maison is a luxury hotel designed for the small mammal with exquisite taste. With access to the finest cuisine, a relaxing spa, and a beach just steps away, your pets will have everything they need to unwind and indulge.

Inspired by the serene beauty of the California coast, we’re setting the new standard in accommodations also open to hamsters. Discover the satisfaction of staying in high-quality comfort, and book your stay at La Petite Maison today.
